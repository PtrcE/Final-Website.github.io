A British Shorthair cat makes a wonderful companion. She is calm and laid-back. The British Shorthair cat is a passionately devoted and affectionate creature that will cling to every member of her household.
Although the British Shorthair enjoys playing, she doesn't require round-the-clock care. She will locate someone and bring them a toy if she is in the mood to play. The British Shorthair performs well alone as well, making her an excellent companion for singles.
