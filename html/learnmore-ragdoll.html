🐾 Ragdoll 🐾
🐾 Personality Traits And Facts 🐾

The Ragdoll is a large, long-bodied cat that gets along well with people and may make a good companion for individuals who live in apartments.




  • Weight Range:


    • Male: Large: >5 kg.
    Female: Large: >5 kg.


    Eye color:
    Blue


  • What To Expect:


    • Energy: Average
    Life Range: 7 - 12 yrs
    Tendency to Shed: High
    Social/Attention Needs: Moderate





  • Coat:

    • Coat Length: Long

    Colors: Red, Frost, Cream, Blue, Chocolate, Lilac, Blue-cream, Seal

    Pattern: Points, Bicolor, Mitted

    Less Allergenic: No

    Grooming Needs: Moderate - High





🐾 Personality 🐾

Although the Ragdoll cat is calm, she does not actually become limp when in your arms. She has a level temperament and gets along with every member of the household. She normally does not become bothered by regular changes. She is the perfect friend for individuals who live in flats.
