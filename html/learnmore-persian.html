The Persian cat is a calm creature that occasionally acts like a young kitten. When she suddenly erupts, running around the room and rolling around, she will be sleeping in the sun.
When she feels like it, the Persian will lie down next to you, take a nap in your bed, and sit on your lap. She is typically pleasant to everyone and does not dislike routine changes.
