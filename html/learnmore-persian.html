🐾 Persian 🐾
🐾 Personality Traits And Facts 🐾

An even-tempered cat, the Persian is known to enjoy a sunny window and show sudden bursts of kitten-like energy.




  • Weight Range:


    • Male: Large: >5 kg.
    Female: Medium: >3 - 5 kg.


    Eye color:
    Blue, Green, Hazel,Copper, Odd-eyed


  • What To Expect:


    • Energy: Average
    Life Range: 8 - 11 yrs
    Tendency to Shed: High
    Social/Attention Needs: Moderate





  • Coat:

    • Coat Length: Long

    Colors: Red, White, Black, Cream, Chocolate, Blue, Lilac,
    Golden, Silver, Cameo, Blue-cream, Tortoiseshell, Calico, Brown, Seal

    Pattern: Tortoiseshell, Bicolor, Solid Color, Tricolor/ Calico,
    Smoke, Tabby, Shaded, Points

    Less Allergenic: No

    Grooming Needs: High





🐾 Personality 🐾

The Persian cat is a calm creature that occasionally acts like a young kitten. When she suddenly erupts, running around the room and rolling around, she will be sleeping in the sun.
When she feels like it, the Persian will lie down next to you, take a nap in your bed, and sit on your lap. She is typically pleasant to everyone and does not dislike routine changes.
