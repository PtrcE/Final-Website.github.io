🐾 Beagle 🐾
🐾 Personality Traits And Facts 🐾

Features: Naturally floppy ears


  • Weight Range:


    • Male: 8 - 13 kg.
    Female: 8 - 13 kg.


  • Height:

    • Male: 15 in.
    Female: 13 in.


  • What To Expect:


    • Exercise: Min. 20 - 40 minutes/day
    Energy: Average
    Life Range: 12 - 15 yrs
    Tendency to Bark: High
    Tendency to Snore: Low
    Tendency to Drool: Low
    Social/Attention Needs: High




  • Bred For: Rabbit / Hare Hunting



  • Coat:

    • Coat Length: Short
    Colors: Any true hound color
    Grooming Needs: Low






🐾 Personality 🐾

Beagles are frequently seen as kind with children and other animals. They are happy dogs who enjoy being petted. However, they like companionship and can become destructive if left alone. One consumer's guide on dogs claims that beagles rank first for excessive barking and might be challenging to housebreak and teach in obedience.
