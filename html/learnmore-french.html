🐾 French Bulldog 🐾
🐾 Personality Traits And Facts 🐾

Features: Brachycephalic (squashed face), short bowed legs. Naturally upright ears


  • Weight Range:

    • Male: 11 - 12 kg.
    Female: 11 - 12 kg.


  • Height:

    • Male: 12 in.
    Female: 11 in.


  • What To Expect:


    • Exercise: Min. 20 minutes/day
    Energy: Average
    Life Range: 9 - 11 yrs
    Tendency to Bark: Moderate
    Tendency to Snore: High
    Tendency to Drool: High
    Social/Attention Needs: High




  • Bred For: Lapdog



  • Coat:

    • Coat Length: Short
    Colors: Black with white or without, fawn, white
    Grooming Needs: Moderate





🐾 Personality 🐾

The French bulldog is a calm home dog who enjoys receiving attention. He even demands it! Given that he could compete with other family members for your attention, this dog is best suited for households with only one person. Only when he finds true cause for excitement does the French bulldog howl frequently.
